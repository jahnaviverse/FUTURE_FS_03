Our Story

A woodland café in the heart of Kakinada.

Vanam — meaning forest in Sanskrit — was born from a simple idea: bring the calm of a deep green canopy into a busy city evening. From single-origin espresso to handmade desserts, every detail invites you to breathe a little slower.

  • Forest-inspiredHanging greenery, warm wood and earthy tones.
  • Crafted with loveEvery cup pulled by hand, every plate seasonal.
  • Quiet luxuryAn unhurried atmosphere where conversations linger.
